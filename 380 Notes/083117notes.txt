Reading 1: The Society of the Spectacle

Background/Main Idea
- Situationist International
  ~ Believe the individual can shape their situation based on their thoughts.
- Marxists - Theory of Alienation
  ~ Anti-capitalist
- Idea of the reading is for the individual to become individualistic
  and unique again, and stray away from the Marxist idea of society.

- "The spectacle is not a collection of images; rather, it is a social
   relationship between people the is mediated by images."

Commodity Fetishism
- The Spectacle is driven by profit

- The spectacle is a worldview that has been actualized;
  translated into an objective force.
